Privacy Policy for Law Review Comment Viewer

Last Updated: December 31, 2024

Data Collection and Storage

Law Review Comment Viewer does not collect, transmit, or store any personal information on external servers.

What We Store Locally:

  • Username: When you first edit a comment, you'll be prompted to enter your name. This is stored only in your browser session and in the Word document's comment data to track who edited which columns.
  • Comment Data: All comment content (text, formatting, table data) is stored exclusively within your Word document using Microsoft Word's native comment system.

What We Don't Collect:

  • We do not collect email addresses, IP addresses, or any personal identifying information
  • We do not track your usage or behavior
  • We do not use cookies or analytics
  • We do not transmit any data to external servers

Data Location

All data remains on your device and in your Word documents. Your comments and edits are never sent to our servers because we don't have any servers.

Third-Party Services

This add-in uses Microsoft's Office.js API to interact with Word. Your use of Microsoft Word and Office 365 is governed by Microsoft's privacy policy, not ours.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted at this URL with an updated "Last Updated" date.
